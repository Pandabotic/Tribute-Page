Kobe Bryant

NBA Legend: The Black Mamba

A Timeline of Kobe Bryant's Career:

  • 1978 - Born in Philidelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Went into the NBA Draft as the first guard out of high school
  • 1997 - Won the Slam Dunk Contest becoming the youngest NBA player to be champion
  • 2000 - Won his first championship with the LA Lakers and would go on a three-peat to win in 2001 and 2002
  • 2006 - Scored a career high of 81 points against the Toronto Raptors
  • 2009 & 2010 - Won back to back championships as well as finals MVP for both
  • 2013 - Underwent season ending injury tearing his achilles
  • 2015 - Final season in the NBA and spent all 20 seasons with the LA Lakers
  • 2018 - Won an Oscar for his animated short film "Dear Basketball"
  • 2020 - Passed Awayat the age of 41 in a fatal helicopter crash
